# Feature: Analytics & Financial Hub
Data-driven oversight for platform health and transparency.

## Metric Logic
- **Revenue**: Recognized at the moment of credit purchase (Cash Basis).
- **Expenses**: Calculated as "Earnings Payable" to tutors upon session completion.
- **Net Profit**: Calculated per currency (PHP & USD) by subtracting payouts from revenue, accounting for regional conversion nuances.

## Admin Dashboards
- **Operational Metrics**: Total bookings, lesson hours, active user counts.
- **Financial Reporting**: Daily/Monthly revenue graphs, pending payout liabilities.
- **Audit Trails**: Detailed logs for credit movements and tutor withdrawals.

## Data Visualization
- Integrated charts and tables for regional performance comparison (e.g., performance in PH vs. International markets).
