# Feature: School Infrastructure
Hierarchical management logic for institutional users and groups.

## The Principal Role
A **Principal** is a managerial role that does not attend sessions themselves but oversees those who do.

## School Profiles
- Instead of individual student accounts, Principals create **Schools**.
- A School profile acts as a central hub for shared credit balances and session schedules.
- Principals can use the **"View as School"** feature to jump into any school's dashboard and act on their behalf.

## Scholarship & Vouchers
- Principals can request and manage bulk **vouchers**.
- These vouchers allow for controlled distribution of credits to students/schools under their supervision.
- Ideal for NGO or Foundation-led tutoring programs.
