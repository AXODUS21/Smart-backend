# Role: Student
**Goal**: Learn and improve through expert tutoring.

### Getting Started
- **Account Setup**: Complete your profile with your name and region (PH/US/Global).
- **Credits**: Purchase credits using **Stripe** (International) or **PayMongo** (GCash/Maya). 
  - *Note: 1 credit = 30 minutes.*

### Core Workflow
- **Find Tutors**: Browse tutors by subject and availability in the "Book Sessions" tab.
- **Booking**: Select a time slot. Credits are deducted immediately upon booking.
- **Meetings**: Join sessions via the meeting link provided in your dashboard or "Calendar" tab.
- **Manage Sessions**: Reschedule or cancel at least **24 hours** in advance to avoid credit forfeiture.
- **Feedback**: Provide feedback for completed sessions to help maintain platform quality.
