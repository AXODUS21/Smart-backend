# Role: Principal (School Admin)
**Goal**: Manage tutoring for institutions or groups.

### Getting Started
- **Manage Schools**: Create one or more "School" profiles to represent different groups or institutions.
- **Student Linking**: Link student accounts to your school for centralized billing and management.

### Core Workflow
- **View as School**: Use the **"View as School"** dropdown in the sidebar to book and manage sessions on behalf of specific schools.
- **Vouchers**: Manage scholarship vouchers and bulk credit assignments for your students.
- **Reporting**: Monitor session hours, attendance, and performance across all linked students in your dashboard.
- **Shared Credits**: Schools manage a central credit pool that can be used by any associated student bookings.
