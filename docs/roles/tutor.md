# Role: Tutor
**Goal**: Share knowledge and earn credits.

### Getting Started
- **Application**: Complete the tutor application with your subjects and experience. Your application must be approved by an Admin before you can start tutoring.
- **Profile**: Keep your profile updated with your expertise to attract more students.

### Core Workflow
- **Availability**: Set your weekly slots in the **Calendar** tab. These are the slots students will see and book.
- **Booking Requests**: Review incoming requests in the "Booking Request" tab. You can accept or decline them.
- **Conducting Sessions**: Launch the meeting room from your **Dashboard** or "Calendar".
- **Earnings & Payouts**: View earned credits and request cash-outs (available on the 15th and 30th of each month) via the "Profile" tab.
- **Assignments**: Manage and review student assignments in the "Assignments" tab.
